About Me

Mechatronics Engineer from Vancouver

I am a Mechatronics Engineer, with a focus area of software and embedded systems. I have one year total of work experience, as a Biomechanical Engineering Research Intern at Human in Motion Robotics, a Product Manager and Developer at MeaningfulWork, and as a Supplier Quality Engineer at Ballard Power Systems.

Aside from my undergraduate studies, I am passionate for student affairs and spent four years with the Mechatronic Systems Engineering Student Society (MSESS), where I was elected as President in my final year. In my four years, I helped plan and lead events, reformed our finances, and led my program through the transition into remote learning during the pandemic, as well as our return to in-person learning. I facilitated accomodations for students with health issues or other concerns during this time, and also introduced new services to the students to keep us connected during this period of isolation.

I also enjoy learning to edit videos and creating 3D models, watching movies and TV shows, skiing, playing basketball, magic tricks, and working out in my free time. I have a YouTube channel where I cook AI-generated recipes and try them with my friends.
