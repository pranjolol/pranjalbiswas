Awards

Undergraduate Leadership Award

Department of Mechatronic Systems Engineering

September 2022

The MSE Undergraduate Leadership Award is awarded at the annual SFU Mechatronics Day event to an undergraduate student who exemplify leadership through involvement with student groups, clubs, professors and classes. The award is given to a student nominated by their peers.

Undergraduate Student Research Award

Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council of Canada (NSERC)

September 2021 - December 2021

The NSERC USRA is funding award to nurture potential for research careers among selected undergraduate students. The award helps encourage and fund research work experience for the awarded student.

Hal Nedler & Cyrus McLean Scholarship

TELUS

September 2021 - December 2021

The Hal Nedler and Cyrus McLean scholarship is a $5000 scholarship awarded by TELUS to students in engineering. Receipients are selected based on community involvment and academic involvement.
